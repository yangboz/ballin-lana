People Analytics Web Demo

People Analytics is a video analytics engine for detecting, tracking, counting, analyzing, and recognizing people in surveillance videos.

It has several use cases:

(1) Customer analytics and service optimization for retail, banking, and hospitality.

(2) Indexing and searching people in surveillance video network.

(3) Digital media management.

(4) Personal media management.

Face Counting

Face Counting can detect faces of people passing through a camera and track them. This can produce a count of the number of people passing through the camera during any time interval. This module can be used for surveillance videos which has clearly visible faces.

Head Counting

Head Counting can detect heads of people passing through a camera and track them. This can produce a count of the number of people passing through the camera during any time interval. This module can be used for surveillance videos mounted on the ceiling. In such situations, the faces are often difficult to detect but heads can be detected reliably.

Face Recognition

Face recognition computes the facial feature vector for each face and compare it with a library of facial feature vectors for identification and verification purposes.

It can operate in several modes:

(1) Still images of known faces are registered.

(2) Each new face in surveillance video is automatically registered.

(3) Faces in a video frame is detected and manually registered.
